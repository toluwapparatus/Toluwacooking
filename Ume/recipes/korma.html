Toluwa-style Lamb Korma

Behold the nigh boundless majesty of this meal! A masterclass in texture and flavor in cuisine, it is usually paired with saffron rice or naan bread. However if you desire to capture the peak Toluwa-experience consuming this meal it is suggested you eat it with both naan (for the overacheivers try Peshuawari!) AND rice.

As for ingredients make sure that you have:

  • 1 .lb lamb steak
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 tablespoon ginger
  • 1½ tablespoon garlic
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • ½ cup coconut milk
  • ¾ cup water
  • Spices
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 tablespoon cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
